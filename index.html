Technical Reference

Material Selection Guide

Choosing the correct elastomer compound is critical to seal performance and service life.

BUNA (NBR)

Nitrile

The most widely used general-purpose sealing compound. Default choice for hydraulic systems, fuel handling, and petroleum-based fluids.

  • Temperature: -40°C to +120°C
  • Petroleum oils and fuels
  • Hydraulic fluids (mineral-based)
  • Water and aqueous solutions
  • Not for ozone / UV exposure
  • Cost-effective for volume

EPDM

Ethylene Propylene

Compound of choice for water, steam, brake fluid, and outdoor applications. Excels where weather resistance and ozone exposure are factors.

  • Temperature: -50°C to +150°C
  • Steam and hot water systems
  • Brake fluids (DOT 3 & 4)
  • Weathering / ozone / UV resistant
  • Not for petroleum oils
  • Outdoor & exposed installs

Viton (FKM)

Fluorocarbon

High-performance option for extreme temperatures and aggressive chemical exposure. Specified where other compounds fail.

  • Temperature: -20°C to +200°C
  • Fuels, solvents, and acids
  • Synthetic lubricants
  • Chemical processing
  • Not for steam / hot water
  • Premium for critical apps
